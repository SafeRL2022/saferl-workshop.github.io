Message from the co-chairs

We sincerely express our sympathy for each person affected by the COVID-19 crisis.

If, unfortunately, the situation has not returned to normal by September, we plan to virtually organize the workshop using a video conference platform that will be communicated to you later.
In all cases, we assure you that all accepted papers will be published by Springer and that the due dates are maintained.

We hope to see you in September!
